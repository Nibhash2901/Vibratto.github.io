How to Play Guitar?

Guitar is one of the most popular instruments that students choose when they first decide to explore the world of music. It has a nice, shallow learning curve and a lot of mass appeals. At Vibratto School of Music, we focus on providing high-quality guitar training in a friendly and supportive environment.

  • Introduction to The Instrument
  • Posture, Grip and Positioning
  • Basic Guitar Techniques
  • Guitar Scales and Rhythm
  • Strumming & Plucking Techniques

How to Western vocals?

Western vocal training can be interesting and challenging at the same time. At Vibratto School of Music, our training methods are highly innovative and enjoyable - from fun and ice-breaking warm-ups to high-energy crescendos. We create a relaxed atmosphere that allows our students to focus on their voice training, along with their own selection of music.

  • Creative Warming up
  • Breathing Techniques for Singing
  • Diction and Articulation Techniques
  • Voice Modulation

How to write a Song?

The singer -songwriter classes give the students an opportunity to be creative & perform their own composed songs in different style. Students will be taught how to enhance their songwriting skills and compose commercially acclaimed songs. Songwriters can accompany themselves with an instrument, have someone accompany them, or use prerecorded music. They will receive feedback from peers and our head trainer.
